    Homes for sale in Seattle, WA have a median listing home price of $838,000. There are 2260 active homes for sale in Seattle, WA, which spend an average of 34 days on the market. Some of the hottest neighborhoods near Seattle, WA are Broadway, Belltown, Wallingford, Fremont, North Admiral.
